# 赤レンガ倉庫事件：画像生成用プロンプト

## 1. 赤レンガ倉庫街の惨状
*   **Prompt**: A dark, foggy, red-brick warehouse district by a canal at night in a dark fantasy city. The alleyways are stained with blood, and the atmosphere is oppressive, terrifying, and grimdark. Broken crates and heavy iron scrap scattered around.
*   **用途**: 記事冒頭、倉庫街の陰惨な雰囲気のイメージ。

## 2. 黒蛇による植物暗殺
*   **Prompt**: A dark fantasy horror illustration of a dead thug's body in a dark alley. Creepy, twisted vines and dark flowers are growing directly out of the flesh of the corpse. The scene is lit by dim, moody lighting, emphasizing the grotesque and magical nature of the assassination.
*   **用途**: 「人間苗床」の恐ろしい呪術暗殺のイメージ。

## 3. 鉄人会による圧死のモニュメント
*   **Prompt**: A grimdark fantasy scene showing a heavy, cracked red-brick wall in a dark alley. Crushed between the bricks and heavy iron plates are torn pieces of dark assassin clothing and dried blood stains, serving as a brutal warning. Dark, oppressive, cinematic lighting.
*   **用途**: 鉄人会による生きたままの壁への塗り込み（圧死）のイメージ。
